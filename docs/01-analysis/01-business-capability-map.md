# Business Capability Map

## Public Portfolio Capabilities

- Home and profile presentation.
- Projects listing and detail pages.
- Blog listing, article detail, RSS, and markdown rendering.
- Resume and digital card pages.
- Contact submission.

## Admin CMS Capabilities

- Dashboard and insights.
- Content CRUD for blog, projects, experience, skills, certificates, education, awards, publications, and speaking.
- Settings and feature flags.
- User management, roles, permissions, sessions, and security posture.
- Messages, audit logs, analytics, communication, and database operations.
