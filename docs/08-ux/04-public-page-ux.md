# Public Page UX

## Experience Goals

Public pages should feel professional, fast, credible, accessible, and recruiter-friendly.

## Content Priorities

Prioritize cybersecurity governance, ISO/IEC 27001:2022, ISMS, risk management, secure SDLC, audit participation, measurable outcomes, projects, publications, and contact pathways.

## Rule

CMS-backed sections should use content models and localization helpers rather than hardcoded copy.
